    • Brief Overview
    Before you enter:
    1. Put on your mask
    2. Check over the list of COVID-19 symptoms
    3. If you answered “Yes” to any of the symptoms DO NOT ENTER the building
    4. If you answered “No” all of the symptoms proceed to enter the building
    When you enter:
    1. Sign in at the front table
    2. Wash your hands
    Volunteer Drivers & Driver Assistants
    1. Look over the donation pick up schedule. Plan route accordingly
    2. Ensure there are a sufficient number of boxes in the van. Load more if necessary
    3. Fill mileage form.
    4. Go on route-Pick up donations
    5. Drop of donations back to Good Measure.
    6. Assist on-site volunteers. Weighing in donations, appropriately storing donations
    7. Look over the client drop-off list. Plan Route accordingly.
    8. Prepare boxes for drop off according to family size, dietary restrictions/allergies & supply.
    Assisting a Client

    Due to COVID-19, we have transitioned to an appointment-based model.

    Volunteer #1
  • The client will ring the doorbell, or you will see them outside
  • Ask for their name, check the SquareSpace Appointments App on the IPad to confirm their name and the appointment time is correct
  • Once their appointment is confirmed:
    1. Ask if they would like to wait outside or inside the building
    2. If they would like to wait inside
    3. Ask them to answer all listed COVID-19 questions
    4. If they answer “Yes” to any of the questions they are unable to come inside the building.
    5. If they answer “No” allow them to come inside the designated waiting area
    6. Ask them to fill out our COVID-19 Tracking sheet
    7. Confirm dietary restrictions/allergies with them
    8. Notify Volunteer #2 of client(s) arrival
    9. State Family Size to Volunteer #2
    10. Alert Volunteer #2 of any dietary restrictions/ allergies
    11. Book in next appointment and converse with client while Volunteer #2 prepares groceries.
    Volunteer #2
  • Place Box on scale and click “Zero” (this will zero out the weight of the box)
  • Prepare groceries according to family size, dietary restrictions & supply of product
  • Weigh out & Document: weigh groceries & document accordingly on “Weight Out” sheet provided
  • Using a shopping cart, wheel out groceries to the client.
  • Sanitize cart in between clients
    • Early/Late Clients
    Early Clients

    If the client is more than 10 minutes early and the current clients’ appointment has not concluded, kindly let the client know you will serve them after.

    Late Clients

    If the client is more than 10 minutes late and another client has arrived at their appointment slot, kindly let the client know that they will have to wait until after the current client's appointment is completed.

    Notes:
  • Kindly remind clients to always arrive at their designated times
  • Appointment times are designated to avoid clusters of people forming
